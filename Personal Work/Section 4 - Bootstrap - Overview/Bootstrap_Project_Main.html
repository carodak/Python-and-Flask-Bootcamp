I love playing table tennis

It is such a cool sport and a lot of people do not know what it takes to play it. You need consistency as it is very easy to miss a shot. You train the brain muscles and you need a strong footwork routine.

Since the table and the paddles are so small, you need to be very precise and hit the ball at the same position. That it also known as timing. Timing is a key concept in table tennis. People tend to think that you do not move a lot. But it is not true. You need powerful stroke and to do so you apply "Power from the ground" concept. You use your whole body to hit the ball. You use your legs, you waist to generate the power.
